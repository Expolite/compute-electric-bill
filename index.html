【 peso per kwh. 】


Formula

Example:
Appliances watt: 20 watt
Daily usage per day: 8 hours
Power rate: 12.10 kWh

(20 watt / 1000 kw) * (8 hours * 30 days) * 12.10 kWh = 58.08 your bill consumption in month.
